Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Important Notice

No data is ever uploaded to any server. All processing happens 100% in your browser. Your files never leave your computer.

Last Updated: December 2023

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. Our converter is designed with privacy as the core principle:

No account registration

You don't need to sign up or create an account

No file uploads

Files stay on your device and are processed locally

No tracking

We don't use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts

No data collection

We don't store any information about you or your files

2. How Our Converter Works

The AppFlowy to Markdown Converter operates entirely client-side:

1
You select files

Choose .json files from your computer or drag & drop

2
Local processing

Files are read directly in your browser using JavaScript

3
In-browser conversion

JSON to Markdown conversion happens on your device

4
Download results

Converted files are downloaded back to your computer

5
Automatic cleanup

All temporary data is cleared when you close the page

3. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services to enhance functionality:

GitHub Pages

For hosting the static website (no server-side processing)

Font Awesome

For icons (loaded from CDN, no access to your data)

JSZip Library

For creating ZIP files (loaded from CDN)

These services may have their own privacy policies, but they don't receive any of your file data.

4. Cookies & Storage

We do not use cookies for tracking. The website may use:

Session storage

To remember your file selections while using the converter (cleared when you close the tab)

Local storage

Optional: To save your preferences like dark mode (you can clear this anytime)

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy.
