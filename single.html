How to Get Your Files

  1. In AppFlowy, go to Settings (⚙️)
  2. Select "Backup" from the menu
  3. Click "Export as ZIP"
  4. Extract the ZIP file on your computer
  5. Find the .json files inside the extracted folder
  6. Upload them here or paste the JSON content
